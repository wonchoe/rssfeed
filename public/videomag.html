Our Story

Petro Mamontov &
Videomag Studio

My name is Petro Mamontov, and I am a wedding videographer and founder of VIDEOMAG STUDIO. Together with my team, we create wedding films throughout Ukraine, Europe, and beyond.

Our work focuses on genuine emotions, atmosphere, and meaningful moments — the details that make each wedding unique. We believe that a wedding film is not just a video, but a story that you will return to for years to come.

We shoot in 4K quality, pay close attention to lighting, composition, and sound, and approach each couple individually. We would be delighted to work with you on your special day!
